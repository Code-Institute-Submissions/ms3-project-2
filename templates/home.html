Purpose

Discrimination happens -
everywhere, anytime.
We need a place to report it -
anywhere, anytime.

Our purpose here is to simply collect data. If you see or experience discrimination then report it. If you are part of a charity, the police or are studying discrimination then please use our database to support your findings.

Data is so important to ensure the battle towards equality continues and does not plateau. So please get involved and let's build a better future, together.

Chris Palmer

About us

We are small team dedicated to giving a voice to those who feel they aren't heard. We are an independent team and have no affiliation with governing bodies. Our dream is to collect information in a way that is free from politics and bureaucracy, and therefore accessible to everyone.

Meet the team

Chris Palmer

A passionate advocate for equal rights - for all people. As a former teacher, Chris has worked hard to promote equality to our children. He now thinks it is time to give everyone a voice - a platform - to speak out."

Disclaimers and usage policy

All statistics are based on the amount of reports in the database. Please interpret any statistics in context and ensure that the results reflect the limited sample size.

Any misrepresentation of the data will be publicly rejected by the company. Any incorrect interpretation of the data will be required to be removed from the public.

Please do not use a person's name in the reports, as the report will be removed.
